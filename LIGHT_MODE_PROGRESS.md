## Design System
- Light: `bg-white` with `text-black`
- Dark: `bg-black` with `text-white`
- Borders: `border-gray-200` (light) / `border-gray-800` (dark)
- Buttons: `bg-gray-100` (light) / `bg-gray-900` (dark)
- Hover: `hover:bg-gray-50` (light) / `hover:bg-gray-900` (dark)
- Text: `text-gray-600` (light) / `text-gray-500` (dark)
